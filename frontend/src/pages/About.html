Who We Are

HoverSprite is a pioneering Vietnamese start-up dedicated to revolutionizing agriculture through advanced drone technology. Based in the Southwest Provinces, we specialize in organic fertilizer and pesticide spraying services for orchards, cereal fields, and vegetable farms.

Our team comprises agricultural experts, technologists, and sustainability advocates committed to enhancing farming efficiency and promoting eco-friendly practices in Vietnam's agricultural sector.

Our Technology

Advanced Drone Fleet

Our fleet of 15 state-of-the-art spraying drones ensures precise and efficient application of organic fertilizers and pesticides. Equipped with GPS technology and advanced sensors, our drones can cover large areas quickly while minimizing overspray and environmental impact.

User-Friendly Booking System

We've developed an intuitive web application that allows farmers to easily book spraying services, track order status, and provide feedback. Our system supports both Gregorian and Lunar calendar dates, respecting local agricultural traditions.
