About us

Who We Are

HoverSprite is a Vietnamese start-up dedicated to revolutionizing agriculture with advanced drone technology. Based in the Southwest Provinces, we specialize in organic fertilizer and pesticide spraying, serving orchards, cereal fields, and vegetable farms.

Our team comprises agricultural experts, technologists, and sustainability advocates committed to enhancing farming efficiency.

Vision

Our vision at HoverSprite is to lead the agricultural sector towards a more sustainable and efficient future. By integrating cutting-edge drone technology with traditional farming practices, we aim to enhance productivity, reduce environmental impact, and support farmers in achieving optimal crop yields. We aspire to be the most reliable partner for farmers and agricultural businesses, setting new standards in agricultural innovation.

Mission

HoverSprite's mission is to provide top-notch spraying services that are accessible to all farmers. We aim to digitize and streamline the spray order process through our user-friendly web application.

Our fleet of advanced drones and expert team ensure precise and timely application of organic fertilizers and pesticides, contributing to healthier crops and better yields. We strive for excellence, continually enhancing our services to stay at the forefront of agricultural technology.
