About HoverSprite

HoverSprite is a leading agricultural solutions company based in the Southwest Provinces of Vietnam. We specialize in organic fertilizer and pesticide spraying services, utilizing a fleet of 15 advanced spraying drones to serve orchards, cereal fields, and vegetable farms.

Our mission is to revolutionize farming practices by providing efficient, eco-friendly, and technology-driven solutions to farmers across the region.

  • Expert sprayers with varying levels of expertise
  • Optimal route planning for efficient service delivery
  • Transparent pricing at 30,000 VND per decare
  • Dual confirmation system for completed services
